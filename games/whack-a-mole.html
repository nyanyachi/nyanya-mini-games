About Whack-a-Mole

Whack-a-Mole is a fast tapping game where one target appears in a 3 x 3 grid. Your goal is to hit the active target as many times as possible before time runs out.

The target keeps moving during the round, so quick focus and accurate taps matter more than random clicking.

How to Play

  1. Press Start Game to begin a 30-second round.
  2. Watch for the active target in one of the nine holes.
  3. Click, tap, or keyboard-activate the active target to earn one point.
  4. Keep scoring until the timer reaches zero.
  5. Use Play Again after the round ends to start fresh.

Rules

Only the active target gives a point. Empty holes do not change the score.

After a successful hit, the target immediately moves to another hole when possible. Scoring is disabled before the round starts and after the game is finished.

Tips and Strategy

  • Keep your pointer or thumb near the center of the grid so every hole is easy to reach.
  • Tap deliberately; empty holes do not help your score.
  • Watch the full board instead of focusing on one corner.
  • On mobile, use short taps to avoid accidental text selection or missed inputs.

Saving and Best Score

Whack-a-Mole saves your best score in this browser using localStorage. A new best is saved when a finished round beats the previous record.

Clearing browser storage may remove the saved best score.

Controls

Use mouse click, touch, or keyboard activation when a hole button is focused. The grid remains visible in every state, and the buttons are sized for desktop and mobile play.
