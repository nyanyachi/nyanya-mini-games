About Apple Clicker

Apple Clicker is a casual score-building game where the goal is to click the apple and grow your current score.

You can spend earned points on upgrades that make each future apple click worth more. It is designed for short sessions, quick replays, and small score challenges.

How to Play

  1. Click or tap the apple to earn points.
  2. Watch your current score increase after each click.
  3. Use points for an upgrade when the upgrade button becomes available.
  4. Keep building your score with your improved points per click.
  5. Try to raise your saved best score over repeated sessions.

Upgrades and Score

Your current score shows the points earned in the active run. Each apple click starts at 1 point per click.

An upgrade costs 50 points from the current run and increases points per click by 1. The best score is the highest score reached in this browser and is saved locally when you pass it.

Tips

  • Consider buying an upgrade once you can afford it, especially if you want a longer run to grow faster.
  • Higher click value helps each future apple click add more points.
  • Short repeated sessions are useful for challenging your previous best score.
  • Use Reset only when you intentionally want to restart the current run.

Saving and Resetting

Apple Clicker saves only the best score in your browser using localStorage. The current score and points per click restart when the page reloads.

The Reset button clears the current run by setting the score back to 0 and points per click back to 1. It does not remove the saved best score. Clearing browser storage may remove the saved best score.

Controls and Accessibility

You can play with a mouse click or by tapping the apple on a touch screen. The apple is a standard button, so browsers may also allow keyboard activation when the button is focused.
