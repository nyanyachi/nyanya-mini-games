About Reaction Test

Reaction Test measures how quickly you respond after the visual signal changes from waiting to ready.

The challenge is to stay ready without clicking too early, then click or tap as soon as the button says Click now. It is built for short, repeatable timing rounds.

How to Play

  1. Press Start to begin a round.
  2. Wait while the button shows Wait...
  3. Do not click during the waiting state.
  4. Click or tap when the button says Click now!
  5. Review your measured time and repeat rounds to compare attempts.

Understanding Your Results

Latest shows the most recent valid reaction time in milliseconds. Lower times mean a faster recorded reaction for that round.

Best shows your fastest saved valid reaction time in this browser. Attempts counts valid completed rounds only.

Early Clicks

If you click before the signal appears, the round stops and the message says it was too early. That early click does not increase the attempts count.

After an early click, the button returns to Start so you can begin a fresh round and wait for the signal again.

Tips for More Consistent Results

  • Keep your pointer or finger ready near the button without pressing early.
  • Focus on the button label so you notice the change to Click now.
  • Use the same device when comparing repeated results.
  • Try several rounds instead of judging your timing from one attempt.

Controls and Devices

You can play with a mouse click or by tapping the button on a touch screen. The reaction control is a standard button, so browsers may also allow keyboard activation when the button is focused.

The layout is designed to remain usable on desktop and mobile browsers.

Saving and Resetting

Reaction Test saves your best time and valid attempt count in your browser using localStorage. The latest time is shown for the current session but is not saved separately.

Reset Stats clears the saved best time and attempts, resets the latest time display, cancels any active waiting timer, and returns the button to Start. Clearing browser storage may also remove saved records.
