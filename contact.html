Contact Nyanya Mini Games

You can contact Nyanya Mini Games about game issues, suggestions, accessibility concerns, privacy or terms questions, or general feedback.

What you can contact us about

Helpful messages may include a game that does not load or work correctly, a button or score that behaves unexpectedly, a sound setting or saved result issue, a broken page link or image, or an idea for improving a game.

Bug report guidance

When possible, please include the game or page name, your device type, browser name, what you expected to happen, what actually happened, and the steps needed to reproduce the issue.

A screenshot can also be helpful if one is available, but it is not required.

Response expectations

Messages are reviewed when possible. A specific response time is not guaranteed, but clear reports and suggestions are appreciated.

Privacy note

Please do not include sensitive personal information in your message. You can read more on the Privacy Policy page.
