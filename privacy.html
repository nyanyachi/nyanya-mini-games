Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Nyanya Mini Games at https://nyanya-mini-games.pages.dev. The games can generally be played without creating an account.

Nyanya Mini Games is built for simple browser play. This page explains what the site may store in your browser and how advertising and hosting services may handle limited technical information.

Information the site may handle

Nyanya Mini Games does not intentionally ask you to submit personal information in order to play the games.

Like most websites, the site's hosting and advertising services may receive standard technical information, such as browser, device, network, or page-request information, when you visit the site.

Browser storage

Some games may use localStorage or similar browser storage to remember small pieces of game data on your device. Examples may include scores, best scores, attempts, game totals, or sound preferences.

Not every game stores every item listed above. Stored game data is kept in your browser and may be removed by clearing browser data or by using in-game reset controls where they are available.

Advertising and Google AdSense

Nyanya Mini Games may display advertising provided by Google AdSense.

Google and its advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, help measure ad performance, and support ad-related features. These tools are managed by Google and its partners according to their own policies and settings.

Third-party services

Nyanya Mini Games is hosted using Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare Pages and Google AdSense may process limited technical data needed to provide hosting, security, advertising, and measurement services.

These third-party services handle information according to their own privacy practices.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about Nyanya Mini Games, please use the Contact page.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the site's features, games, or services change.
